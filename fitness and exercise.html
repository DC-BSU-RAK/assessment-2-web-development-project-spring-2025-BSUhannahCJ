INTRODUCTION

For us humans, it is essential for us to move because that is how we were built. But the way we treat our bodies through physical activity can affect both our physical and mental well-being. The right amount of exercise and physical movement will improve our health, physique, and even mental health! Remember, balance is key. Too little or too much exercise can both have consequences.

DIFFERENT FORMS OF EXERCISE

There are various ways to move our bodies; multiple forms of fitness and exercise. This is because different types of exercises targets different parts of the body and different forms of strengths. Here are a few examples!


Walking

a simple but powerful form of exercise. It improves cholesterol levels, lifts your mood, and streghtens your bones.

Yoga

This is a form of exercise that combines physical, mental, and spiritual disciplines. Helps you gain control over your body and mind.

Strength Training

Weight lifting, power lifting, and bodyweight exercises are a few examples of strength training. as the name suggets, in this form of exercise, you build your strength and metabolism by training and disciplining your body in a specific matter.

Tai Chai

Tai chai is a Chinese martial arts and a form of exercise. Tai chai combines movements and relaxation that improves both your body and mind. It's accessible for all ages.
