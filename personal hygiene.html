INTRODUCTION

Maintaining our personal hygiene is an essential because it's a significant part of how we stay healthy and presentable. Habits such as brushing our teeth, showering, and grooming, like taking care of our skin and hair, has a direct impact on both our physical health and mental confidence. Having good hygiene helps prevent illness and boosts self-esteem. Consistency is key.
